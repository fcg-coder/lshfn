Upon entering the world, the user is first introduced to an amorphous and abstract space, which bears a striking resemblance to internal organs or a rudimentary living organism. This space is characterized by its enveloping and slippery nature, with its partially filled water and dynamic fractal objects serving to create an atmosphere of non-human habitation in the virtual realm. Concurrently, the user experiences a sense of being in a secure and comforting space, reminiscent of a womb, while also feeling a sense of being in an unexplored and extraterrestrial closed organ that has (or not) yet to be claimed.
